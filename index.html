Overview

OnePDF+ (“the App”) is an offline PDF toolkit for Android. Your documents are processed on your device. We do not operate cloud servers that receive or store your PDF files, images, or converted documents.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit to our servers, or store:

  • Contents of PDFs, images, or Word documents you open or create
  • Filenames or folder paths (except locally on your device to save outputs you request)
  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information

Information Processed on Your Device

The following stays on your device:

  • Files you select through the system file picker
  • Output files saved to In-app Documents (merged, edited, compressed, split, Word exports)
  • Edits in the PDF Editor (highlights, drawings, text, signatures, page changes)

You choose when to share or save elsewhere using Android’s share sheet or save dialog. We do not receive those files when you share them.

App Permissions

  • Storage / files — select and save documents via the system picker (you choose files)
  • Network / internet — load and display advertisements; PDF tools work offline

The App does not request contacts, microphone, camera, or location for core features.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays banner advertisements on the home screen through Google AdMob (Google LLC). When ads are shown, Google may collect information as described in:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob policies
  • How Google uses partner data

This may include device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data. We do not receive your PDF or document content through advertising.

Limit ad personalization via Settings → Google → Ads on Android. If offline, ads may not load; PDF tools still work.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — display ads
  • Google Play services — required for AdMob on Android

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Contact us if you believe a child has provided personal information.

Data Retention

  • Your documents — on your device until you delete them or uninstall the App
  • Ad-related data — retained per Google’s policies

Security

Files are processed locally. We do not transmit document content to our servers. You are responsible for securing your device and backups.

Your Choices

  • Delete outputs from My Documents anytime
  • Share files only when you explicitly choose Share or Save as
  • Manage ad personalization in Android/Google settings
  • Uninstall the App to remove its private app data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and change the effective date above. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
